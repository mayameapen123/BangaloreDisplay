Bangalore Typeface

Bangalore Display is a Kannada typeface inspired by an analysis of
the bold, bottom heavy hand lettered signs peppered across Bangalore, India. The typeface combines aspects of the hand lettered signs with subtle influences drawn from strokes created with an ice-cream stick.

The reference images were traced to analyze the stroke width, bowl width, terminals and other stylistic qualities
